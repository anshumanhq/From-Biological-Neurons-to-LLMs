# Neural Network History – Master Chronology (1943–2026)

| Year | Paper / Event | Significance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1943 | McCulloch & Pitts | First artificial neuron model (threshold logic) |
| 1949 | Hebb | Synaptic plasticity (Hebbian learning) |
| 1950 | Turing | Turing Test, child machine concept |
| 1958 | Rosenblatt | Perceptron – first trainable neural network |
| 1960 | Widrow & Hoff | ADALINE, LMS algorithm (gradient descent) |
| 1969 | Minsky & Papert | Perceptrons – single-layer limitations |
| 1974 | Werbos | Backpropagation proposed (PhD thesis) |
| 1980 | Fukushima | Neocognitron – CNN precursor |
| 1982 | Hopfield | Energy-based recurrent networks |
| 1986 | Rumelhart, Hinton & Williams | Backpropagation popularised |
| 1989 | LeCun | CNN applied to digit recognition |
| 1990 | Jordan | Recurrent network with output feedback |
| 1991 | Elman | Simple RNN with hidden-state recurrence |
| 1997 | Hochreiter & Schmidhuber | LSTM – solves vanishing gradients |
| 1998 | LeCun et al. | LeNet-5 – mature CNN architecture |
| 1999 | Gers, Schmidhuber & Cummins | Forget-gate LSTM introduced (adaptive resetting) |
| 2000 | Gers et al. | "Learning to Forget" – forget gate formalised |
| 2006 | Hinton et al. | Deep Belief Networks – deep learning revival |
| 2012 | Krizhevsky et al. | AlexNet – ImageNet breakthrough |
| 2014 | Sutskever et al. | Seq2Seq – neural machine translation |
| 2014 | Goodfellow et al. | GAN – generative adversarial networks |
| 2015 | He et al. | ResNet – very deep networks |
| 2017 | Vaswani et al. | Transformer – self-attention |
| 2018 | Devlin et al. | BERT – bidirectional pre-training |
| 2018 | Radford et al. | GPT – generative pre-training |
| 2019 | Radford et al. | GPT-2 – scaling language models |
| 2020 | Brown et al. | GPT-3 – few-shot learning at scale |
| 2022 | Ouyang et al. | InstructGPT / RLHF – alignment |
| 2023 | OpenAI | GPT-4 – advanced reasoning |
| 2024–2026 | Various | Agentic AI, multimodal, RAG |
