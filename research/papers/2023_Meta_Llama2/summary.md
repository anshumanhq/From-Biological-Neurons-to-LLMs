# Summary: Llama 2: Open Foundation and Fine-Tuned Chat Models

**Authors:** Hugo Touvron et al.  
**Year:** 2023

Llama 2 extended the LLaMA family with models up to 70B parameters, including chat‑fine‑tuned versions (Llama 2‑Chat) aligned with RLHF. The models were released under a permissive licence, enabling commercial use. The paper introduced a comprehensive RLHF pipeline with safety‑specific reward models and explored the trade‑offs between helpfulness and safety. Llama 2 set a new standard for open‑weight foundation models.
