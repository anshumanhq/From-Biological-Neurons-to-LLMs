# Timeline Context: Rosenblatt (1958)

- **Preceded by:**
  - 1943: McCulloch & Pitts (formal neuron)
  - 1949: Hebb (synaptic learning rule)
  - 1950: Turing (child machine / learning philosophy)
  - 1957: Rosenblatt begins building the Mark I Perceptron.

- **Contemporaries:**
  - 1959: Widrow's ADALINE (linear adaptive filter, LMS algorithm).
  - 1960: Selfridge's Pandemonium model (parallel processing of features).

- **Followed by:**
  - 1969: Minsky & Papert's *Perceptrons* (the first AI winter trigger).
  - 1974: Werbos proposes backpropagation (PhD thesis).
  - 1986: Rumelhart, Hinton & Williams resurrect multi-layer networks.
