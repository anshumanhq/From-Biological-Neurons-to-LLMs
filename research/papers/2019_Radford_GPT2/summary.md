# Summary: Language Models are Unsupervised Multitask Learners

**Authors:** Alec Radford, Jeffrey Wu, Rewon Child, David Luan, Dario Amodei, Ilya Sutskever  
**Year:** 2019

GPT‑2 demonstrated that scaling a Transformer language model to 1.5 billion parameters and training on a large, diverse web corpus (WebText, 40GB) could produce strong zero‑shot task performance without task‑specific fine‑tuning. The model family included four sizes: Small (117M), Medium (345M), Large (774M), and XL (1.5B). The paper introduced byte‑level BPE tokenization and established language modelling as a general‑purpose learning framework.
