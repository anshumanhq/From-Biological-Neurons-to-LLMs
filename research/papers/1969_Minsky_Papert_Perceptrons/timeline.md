# Timeline Context: Minsky & Papert (1969)

- **Before:**
  - 1958: Rosenblatt publishes the Perceptron.
  - 1960: Widrow & Hoff publish ADALINE/LMS.
  - 1963: Papert joins MIT; collaboration begins.
- **Publication:**
  - 1969: *Perceptrons* is published by MIT Press.
  - 1972: Hand‑corrected edition.
- **After:**
  - 1970s: First AI Winter; neural network research declines.
  - 1986: Rumelhart, Hinton & Williams publish backpropagation.
  - 1988: Expanded edition with new chapter responding to critics.
  - 2017: Reissue with foreword by Léon Bottou.
