# Summary: Perceptrons

**Authors:** Marvin Minsky, Seymour Papert  
**Year:** 1969  
**ISBN:** 0-262-13043-2

Minsky and Papert provided a rigorous mathematical analysis of single-layer Perceptrons, proving that they cannot compute XOR, parity, or connectedness. The book's critique was widely interpreted as a death knell for neural networks, triggering the First AI Winter. However, the authors explicitly left open the possibility of multi-layer networks—a challenge that would be answered by backpropagation in the 1980s.
