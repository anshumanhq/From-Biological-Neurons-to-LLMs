# Timeline Context: Werbos (1974)

- **Before:**
  - 1960: Widrow & Hoff (LMS/Delta Rule)
  - 1969: Minsky & Papert (Perceptrons)
- **Publication:**
  - 1970: Linnainmaa publishes reverse-mode AD (independent, but not for neural networks)
  - 1974: Werbos submits his PhD thesis
- **After:**
  - 1986: Rumelhart, Hinton & Williams popularise backpropagation
  - 1994: Werbos publishes *The Roots of Backpropagation*
