# Summary: Beyond Regression

**Author:** Paul John Werbos  
**Year:** 1974

Werbos proposed backpropagation—the recursive application of the chain rule to compute gradients in multi-layer networks—in his PhD thesis. The algorithm is a generalisation of the LMS rule to networks with hidden layers. While largely ignored for over a decade, this work provided the mathematical foundation for the backpropagation revolution of the 1980s.
