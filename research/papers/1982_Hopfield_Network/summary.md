# Summary: Neural Networks and Physical Systems

**Author:** John J. Hopfield  
**Year:** 1982  
**DOI:** 10.1073/pnas.79.8.2554

Hopfield introduced an energy-based recurrent neural network for associative memory. The network stores patterns as attractors using a Hebbian rule, and the asynchronous update dynamics always converge to a local minimum of an energy function. This paper bridged neural networks with statistical physics, proving that neural networks were scientifically legitimate and attracting physicists to the field.
