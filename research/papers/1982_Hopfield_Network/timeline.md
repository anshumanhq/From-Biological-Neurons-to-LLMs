# Timeline Context: Hopfield (1982)

- **Before:**
  - 1949: Hebb (correlation rule)
  - 1969: Minsky & Papert (Perceptron critique)
  - 1974: Werbos (backpropagation proposed)
- **Contemporaries:**
  - 1983: Cohen & Grossberg (continuous Hopfield)
  - 1985: Ackley, Hinton & Sejnowski (Boltzmann Machines)
- **After:**
  - 1986: Rumelhart, Hinton & Williams (backpropagation popularised)
  - 2020: Ramsauer et al. (Modern Hopfield Networks = Transformers)
  - 2024: Hopfield awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics (with Hinton)
