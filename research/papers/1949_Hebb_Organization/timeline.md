# Timeline Context: Hebb (1949)

- **Preceded by:**
  - 1906: Ramon y Cajal receives Nobel Prize (Neuron Doctrine).
  - 1943: McCulloch & Pitts publish their formal logic neuron.
  
- **Contemporaries:**
  - 1948: Wiener publishes *Cybernetics*.
  - 1950: Turing publishes *Computing Machinery and Intelligence*.

- **Followed by:**
  - 1958: Rosenblatt's Perceptron (uses Hebbian principles explicitly).
  - 1973: Bliss & Lømo experimentally discover Long-Term Potentiation (LTP), validating Hebb's synapse in the hippocampus.
