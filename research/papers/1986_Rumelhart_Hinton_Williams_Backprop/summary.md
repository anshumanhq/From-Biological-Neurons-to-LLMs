# Summary: Learning Representations by Back-propagating Errors

**Authors:** David E. Rumelhart, Geoffrey E. Hinton, Ronald J. Williams  
**Year:** 1986  
**DOI:** 10.1038/323533a0

This paper popularised backpropagation, providing a clear derivation and compelling experiments (XOR, encoder, family tree). It demonstrated that multi-layer networks could learn useful internal representations, solving the XOR problem that had plagued Perceptrons. The paper triggered the connectionist revival and laid the foundation for modern deep learning.
