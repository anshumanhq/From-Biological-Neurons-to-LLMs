# Summary: ImageNet Classification with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks

**Authors:** Alex Krizhevsky, Ilya Sutskever, Geoffrey E. Hinton  
**Year:** 2012

AlexNet demonstrated that deep CNNs with ReLU activations, dropout, data augmentation, and GPU training could achieve breakthrough performance on the ImageNet challenge. It reduced the top‑5 error rate from 26% to 15.3%, sparking the modern deep‑learning revolution.
