# Summary: Generative Adversarial Nets

**Authors:** Ian J. Goodfellow, Jean Pouget-Abadie, Mehdi Mirza, Bing Xu, David Warde-Farley, Sherjil Ozair, Aaron Courville, Yoshua Bengio  
**Year:** 2014

GANs introduced adversarial training as a general framework for learning generative models through competition between a generator and discriminator. The minimax objective \( \min_G \max_D V(D,G) \) connects the training dynamics to Jensen-Shannon divergence minimization. This paper established a new paradigm for generative modelling that has since been extended to image, text, and audio generation.
